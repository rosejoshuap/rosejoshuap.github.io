Tyler Scott Illusionist

Tyler Scott is an up and coming magician and needs a website so potential clients can easily learn about him and book gigs. Because he's only been in the business for a few years, there isn’t much content in terms of photos or reviews to put on his site.

To make make the most of what he does have, I took the less-is-more approach - using a single page format with a large, striking banner. This way, I can focus the client's attention to Tyler’s demo reel and contact form, which are the two most important elements of the website. There is just enough information to spurn the client’s interest, but the rest is a mystery. Kind of fitting for a magician!
